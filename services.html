// 03 — Privacy Engineering

Privacy is architecture, not policy.

We don't bolt privacy on at the end. It's woven into the foundations of every product — local-first, encrypted, transparent.

"If the data never leaves the device, the device becomes the perimeter." — wangchenggroup design principle

On-Device Encryption

AES-256 with keys held in Secure Enclave. User data stays encrypted even at rest — and never crosses the network unless explicitly required.

Zero Cloud by Default

Our products are offline-first by default. Sync is opt-in, end-to-end encrypted, and never the only path to access user data.

Compliance by Design

GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, PIPL, LGPD — every product ships with documentation, age-gating, consent flows, and data export tools baked in.

Transparent Tracking

We never track across apps, never fingerprint devices, and never sell user data. Our analytics are aggregated, opt-in, and disclosed in plain English.

Data Portability

Export everything in standard formats. Delete in one tap. We believe ownership means portability — your data, your call, your timeline.

Audit Trails

Every privacy-relevant action is logged locally — visible to the user. We don't hide the seams; we celebrate the transparency.

// 05 — Cross-Market Localization

Built for global launch.

Compliance-aware builds for European and American markets — every policy, every rating, every consent flow handled.

🇪🇺 GDPR & ePrivacy

Lawful basis processing, right-to-erasure workflows, data portability exports, consent management, DPO contact information, and Privacy by Design documentation.

🇺🇸 CCPA / CPRA

California Consumer Privacy Act compliance — opt-out of sale, do-not-sell links, consumer rights request handling, and verifiable consumer requests workflow.

🇺🇸 COPPA

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act — verifiable parental consent, age gating, restricted data collection, and safe harbor provisions for under-13 users.

🇨🇳 PIPL & 🌍 LGPD

Personal Information Protection Law (China) and Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (Brazil) — cross-border transfer assessments and consent records.

🇪🇺 DSA & DMA

Digital Services Act and Digital Markets Act — transparency reports, content moderation disclosures, and interoperability considerations.

📱 App Store Compliance

App Store Connect age ratings, privacy nutrition labels, IDFA / ATT compliance, in-app purchase disclosure, and regional store availability management.

// 06 — How We Work

A disciplined process.

Six phases, each gated by review. Predictable timelines, transparent pricing, no surprises.
